Feel Free to AskQuestions

Q: How can I cancel a reservation?
A: Inform the driver by phone or email at least 24 hours before your scheduled reservation.

Q: What are the hourly rates?
A: Hourly rates are a pay by the hour service and are only available for service entirely within King County. There is a Two hour minimum for hourly rates.

Q: How will I be picked up at the airport?
A: Take note of the baggage claim door number, written above the large sliding exit doors. Call your driver and tell him this number. Wait outside of baggage claim by the curbside, and the driver will arrive in less than five minutes and greet you by name. Make sure to wait to be greeted.

Q: Wait time?
A: The driver frequently checks flight status based on your flight information in order to arrive promptly. Customers are allowed 30 minutes complimentary wait time.
